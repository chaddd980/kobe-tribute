  • . Born August 23, 1978, to former NBA player Joe 'Jellybean'Bryant
  • . Started playing basketball at the age of 3 years old
  • . Played high school basketball at Lower Marion high school in Philadelphia
  • . In his senior year he led his team to its first state championship in 53 years with a record of 31-3, while averaging 30.8 points, 12 rebounds, 6.5 assists, 4.0 steals, and 3.8 blocks and was named the Naismith High School Player of the Year, Gatorade Men's National Basketball Player of the Year, a McDonald's All-American and a USA Today All-USA first Team Player
  • . Bryant skipped college and declared directly for the NBA draft from highschool
  • . He was drafted 13th by the Charlotte Hornets and traded to the Los Angeles Lakers in the 1996 draft
  • .Over his career he averaged 25 points, 5.2 rebounds and 4.7 assists, amassed 15 all nba selections 12 all nba defensive selections and 4 time all-star mvp
  • . He is the second in all star appearances at 18 and third in all time scoring behind Karl Malone and Kareem Adbul Jabbar

  • . He was the first guard to play 20 seasons in the NBA and all with the Lakers
